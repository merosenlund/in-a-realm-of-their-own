# Project Roadmap: In a Realm of Their Own

This document tracks the high-level progression of the project. For specific feature details, see [featureBacklog.md](./featureBacklog.md).

## Phase 1: Foundation (COMPLETED)
- [x] Basic 3D First-Person Movement.
- [x] Autonomous Realm Expansion (Growth Radius).
- [x] Realm Core: Central mana generator (Floating Orb).
- [x] Basic HUD for Mana display.

## Phase 2: Interaction & The Market
- [ ] **Raycasting Interaction**: Detecting blocks and the Realm Core.
- [ ] **Mining & Building**: Breaking blocks (inventory) and placing them.
- [ ] **The Store**: UI to purchase blocks with Mana.
- [ ] **Resource Items**: Blocks dropped as physical items or added to inventory.

## Phase 3: Mana States - Phase A (Gas)
- [ ] **Mana Harvesting**: Collecting "Ambient Gas" during mining.
- [ ] **Mana Vents**: Specific world locations with high gas density.
- [ ] **Piping System**: Basic pipes to move Mana Gas from collectors to "Mana Jars" or simple machines.

## Phase 4: Mana States - Phase B (Liquid & Solid)
- [ ] **Liquifier Structure**: Compressing Gas into Liquid (requires mana/power).
- [ ] **Liquid Piping**: More complex fluid management.
- [ ] **Solidifier**: Creating Mana Crystals/Bricks from Liquid.
- [ ] **Energy Density**: Implementing different "burn times" or "power levels" for each state.

## Phase 5: Realm Expansion & Discovery
- [ ] **The Teleporter**: Using Solid Mana to fuel inter-realm jumps.
- [ ] **Shard Generation**: Creating temporary resource realms.
- [ ] **Persistence**: Saving and loading multiple realms.

## Phase 6: Automation & Meta
- [ ] **Conveyor Belts**: Moving Solid Mana and items.
- [ ] **Mana-Powered AI**: Small drones or golems to help maintain the realm.
- [ ] **Realm Integration**: Merging shards into your Home Realm.
